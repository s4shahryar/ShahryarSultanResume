## What we do

We provide complete Microsoft Dynamics 365 and Power Platform services. Whether you need a full CRM
implementation, a single integration, or senior architecture guidance, we cover the entire delivery
lifecycle.

### Dynamics 365 CRM implementation
End-to-end implementation of Dynamics 365 Customer Engagement - including **Sales, Customer Insights,
and Project Operations**. We handle requirements gathering, configuration, customisation, data
migration, testing, and go-live, delivering production-ready systems aligned to your business
processes.

### Solution architecture and advisory
Senior, architecture-led guidance for new builds and existing systems. We define scalable solution
designs, review and de-risk in-flight projects, and act as the single point of contact between
business stakeholders and technical teams.

### Power Platform development
Low-code and pro-code delivery on the Microsoft Power Platform: **Model-Driven Apps, Canvas Apps,
Power Automate, and Dataverse**. We automate business processes and build apps that extend Dynamics
365 and standalone line-of-business needs.

### System and Azure integration
Reliable integration between Dynamics 365 and your wider technology landscape using **Azure Logic
Apps, APIs, and custom services**. We connect CRM to ERP, finance, billing, and third-party platforms
with resilient, high-throughput integration layers.

### Custom development and extension
Tailored business logic with **C# .NET plugins, workflows, and custom activities**, plus client-side
extensions in **JavaScript and TypeScript**, for requirements that go beyond configuration - across
both online and on-premise environments.

### Data migration and reporting
Data migration, transformation, and reporting using **SSIS, SSRS, and SQL Server**, ensuring clean,
trustworthy data and the insight your teams need.

### Support, DevOps, and managed delivery
Ongoing support and troubleshooting for production systems, plus structured deployment and release
management with **Azure DevOps and CI/CD pipelines** for predictable, continuous delivery.

## Featured project: end-to-end CRM for Power and Data Management (PDM)

We delivered a complete, end-to-end Microsoft Dynamics 365 CRM implementation for
[Power and Data Management (PDM)](https://pdmllc.net/), a US-based engineered-to-order manufacturer of
power transformers, dead tank circuit breakers, and gas-insulated switchgear serving data centers,
hyperscalers, utilities, and telecom carriers.

The engagement covered the full delivery lifecycle - requirements gathering, solution design,
configuration and customisation, integration, testing, and go-live - resulting in a production-ready
Dynamics 365 CRM platform aligned to PDM's sales and operations processes.
